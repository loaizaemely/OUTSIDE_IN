NEUROCRANE

It is the portion of the skull that covers, supports and protects the brain and its membranous coverings: the meninges, which are the three thin layers of tissue that cover and protect the brain.

The neurocranium is divided into 8 bones and has a main subdivision into two structures that constitute the neurocranial roof and floor: the base and the vault.

PRINCIPAL FUNCTIONS

° Shape the head.

° Protects the brain, organs and areas of the brain that control the five senses.

VAULT

It is the space in the skull within the neurocranium, occupied by the brain and is made up of the frontal squama, the parietals, the upper portion of the occipital squama, the temporal squama and the greater wings of the sphenoid.
They are flat bones with a configuration histological configuration of two superficial compact bone tables, internal and external, and between them a layer of spongy bone known as diploe .

BASE

It is composed of five bones: frontal, ethmoid, sphenoid, temporal and occipital; three naturally contoured regions can be identified when viewed from above, the anterior, middle, and posterior cranial fossa.
This involves several functions such as:
° Promote the articulation of the head with the neck.
° Provide a support for the brain and a platform from which the facial skeleton grows.
° Articulate the mandible with the skull.
° Accommodate the pharynx.

VISCEROCRANIAL

Also known as the facial mass, it is a collection of bones that form the facial skeleton, it receives its name in contrast to the neurocranium. Is situated inferior to the anterior half of the skull and consists of the bones that surround the mouth, nose, nasal cavity, and most of the eye socket. It is divided into 2 main parts: The upper jaw and the lower jaw.

PRINCIPAL FUNCTIONS

° Gives the characteristic shape to the human face.
° Protects the delicate organs of the face.
° Provides a bony surface for the attachment of facial muscles.
° Contains foramina for the passage of neurovascular structures.
° Protects the structures of the face.
