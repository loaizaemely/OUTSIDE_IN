AXIAL SKELETON

The axial skeleton includes all the bones along the longitudinal axis of the body.

This skeleton includes the bones that form the bony structure of the head, the auditory bones, the laryngeal skeleton, the spinal column, and the rib cage.

FUNCTIONS

The axial skeleton depends on the protection and support of the different internal organ systems.
As well as the interior of the skull and the spinal column where the central nervous system is located, in addition to the supports of:

째 The eye sockets
째 The nasal cavity
째 The jaws and mouth
째 The tympanic cavity
In the thorax or trunk are the cardiovascular and respiratory systems.
